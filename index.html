About Me

I was born in kerala, India in 1991. I received my be degree in electronics and communication engineering in the year 2013. In the beginning when I was a kid, the subjects of maths and physics have always fascinated me. As I progressed through school I found I was much more interested in the application of the theory and rather than the theory itself and this is the reason behind my engineering. I also had interest in doing advance studies in computer application and networking and so im pursuing my masters in information technology in Charles Sturt University Melbourne, Australia.

Apart from my academic, I always been interested in photography. It was just an interest in the beginning and then it became my passion. I found my first love for photography on a family holiday to africa. For me, photography is about the concept ,the reasoning behind the image is far more than the aesthetic value. Having the passion for photography is the only reason which took me half way across the country to where I reside now from where I grew up.
